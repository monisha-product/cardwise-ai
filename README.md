# CardWise AI - Product Vision

## Live Demo

Try the MVP here: [CardWise AI](https://cardwise-ai.vercel.app/)

## Vision

Help users maximize the value of every transaction by recommending the best credit card based on rewards, cashback, benefits, and spending milestones.

---

## Problem Statement

Many users own multiple credit cards but do not know which card should be used for a particular purchase.

Credit card reward structures vary across:

* Categories
* Merchants
* Spending thresholds
* Cashback rates
* Reward programs

As a result, users often lose potential value.

---

## Target Users

### Frequent Travelers

Users who own travel-focused cards and want to maximize miles and travel rewards.

### Cashback Seekers

Users focused on maximizing cashback across daily spending.

### Multi-Card Users

Users who own multiple credit cards and want a simple recommendation engine.

---

## Product Goal

Provide an instant recommendation for the best card to use for any transaction.

Input:

* Amount
* Category
* Merchant

Output:

* Recommended Card
* Estimated Reward Value
* Recommendation Reason

---

## Success Criteria

Users receive accurate and understandable card recommendations within seconds.

---

## Future Vision

Become a personal financial rewards assistant that:

* Tracks spending automatically
* Monitors milestones
* Suggests new cards
* Optimizes reward earnings across all spending
